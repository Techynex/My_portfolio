Project Title: TamiNadu- Cyber Crime and Criminal Analysis System (GOvernment)

Developed a Cyber Crime and Criminal Analysis System for Tamil Nadu, improving data visualization and insights. Analyzed datasets to identify critical patterns for better decision-making

Live Link

Project Overview

In this project, I developed a Cyber Crime and Criminal Analysis System for Tamil Nadu, aimed at enhancing data visualization and insights. I began by aggregating and preprocessing diverse datasets related to cyber crimes and criminal activities. Through exploratory data analysis and feature engineering, I identified key patterns and trends that could inform better decision-making.

To provide actionable insights, I utilized advanced data visualization techniques, creating intuitive dashboards and reports that highlighted critical patterns and anomalies. The system enabled law enforcement and decision-makers to quickly understand and respond to emerging threats, improving overall efficiency in crime analysis and management.
