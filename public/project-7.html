Project Title: Speech Emotion Recognition

Created a AI Based Module that can be used to understand the emotion of a particular person on the basis of his or her speech which basically monitors the persons speech and determines his emotion on the basis of training module.

Live Link

Project Overview

I created an AI-based module to analyze and understand emotions based on a person's speech. This project involved developing a system that monitors and evaluates speech to determine emotional states using a trained model and trained model was also newly created by me.

he module utilizes advanced speech processing techniques and machine learning algorithms to analyze various features of the speech, such as tone, pitch, and tempo. By training the model on a diverse dataset of emotional speech samples, the system can accurately classify and identify emotions in real-time. This capability provides valuable insights into a person's emotional state, enhancing applications in customer service, mental health monitoring, and interactive systems.
